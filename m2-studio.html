Design Brief

In 2050s, people live in a more decentralized community. They don’t need to squeeze in a mega city to seek for a high quality life, work, education, service or pruchase. People are augmented to specialize in some fields and they have more free time to live by sparkles. Everythings are more accessible in suburbs and towns.

Design a transportation system which could help bringing mega city function to a suburb(Monterey Park).

Key Insight

3 Key Steps to support the Assumption

  1. Decentralize the mega city, logistically and functionally
  2. Great IoV to support logistics system and transportation system to decrease mobile cost
  3. Transport system supporting local business
